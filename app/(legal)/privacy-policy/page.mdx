# Privacy Policy for To Ghibli

## Introduction

Welcome to To Ghibli, the **AI-powered Ghibli-style image generator** that transforms your photos into magical Ghibli-style art. At To Ghibli, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using To Ghibli:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, track your credits, and provide customer support.

2. **Image Data**

   - **What We Collect**: The images you upload for Ghibli-style transformation and the generated Ghibli-style images.
   - **Purpose**: To provide our core service of transforming your images into Ghibli-style art.

3. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use To Ghibli, including your image generation history, style preferences, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our services, and provide personalized recommendations.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access To Ghibli, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our premium services.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
